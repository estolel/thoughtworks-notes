Concept Maps 
 - Graphical Tools
 - Representing concept-propositional or meaning frameworks

Concept Map Parts
 - Concepts - Circles/Boxes
 - Relationships - Connecting line
 - Linking phrases/words
 - Propositions 
   - Statements about an object or event
   - Contains two or more concepts connected by linking words/phrases to form a meaningful statement.
 -Focus Question - Specifies the topic. Needs to have a "what,who,how,where,why,etc" to make the concept map specific.

Building a Concept Map(app.diagrams.net):
-Determine Focus Question
-Identify Key Concepts
-Rank order Concepts
-Connect Concepts
-Modify Structure
-Add Examples
