CODE REVIEW
-Allow other developers to look at code smells and potentially FATAL mistakes. 
Example: DTO->DB was empty

STANDUP MEETINGS
-Teams discuss the progress of stories. This allows everyone in the team to be in the loop. Some announcements are also made here such as future plans

PAIRED PROGRAMMING
-Developers work together on stories so that they can help each other when one gets blocked. Example: tilted

IPM
-The BA and QA discuss stories that are to be worked on during the iteration. The devs discuss how much effort they will pour into a story

Team Retro
-Teams allow everyone to speak their mind and share their troubles so that the whole team can provide a solution. 
Example: assignment solutions
